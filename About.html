Usage

This website is used to calculate reflections on different senarios and track whale movement. Insert you desired holdings in "amount" and fill then play around with the rest of the input fields.

How to use calculator

  1. Enter your holdings in "Amount"
  2. Fill "Daily volume"
  3. Enter the Supply, where you think the burn will stop
  4. Enter the Price
  5. Note: By clicking on "Burn" or "Static" you are able to switch between the two options
  6. Note: The "Million" / "Billion" / "Trillion" beside the inputs can be switched as you desire

Math

The math used to calculate reflections are based on Safemoon Mark aka Defi Mark's formula:
(Volume * Balance) / (20 * Supply)
